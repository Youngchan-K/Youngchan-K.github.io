About Me

I'm a ML Engineer at Beeble, where I improve the performance of human portrait relighting model, SwitchLight. I'm interested in controllable content generation and editing, empowering general users to create high-quality visuals for storytelling with streamlined and user-friendly workflow.

Previously, I completed my Master's degree at POSTECH Computer Graphics Lab under the supervision of Prof. Seung-Hwan Baek. My graduate research focused on comprehensive analysis and modeling of light properties [NeSpoF, SpoDataset, Spectro-polarimetric BRDF].
